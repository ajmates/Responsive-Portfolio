About Me

Breakfast fanatic, obssesd with my dog, Jax (yes, short for Flap Jax)! Sundays are for pancakes. Always planning my next meal. Worst fear: going somewhere, getting hungry and I have no food.

I believe that a pinky promise is legally binding. I'm at my most insecure when I have to spell the word queso. Strong advocate that tacos aren't just for Tuesdays. Convinced that chocolate and/or peanut butter could heal the world.
